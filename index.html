About Me

Heather (Cormier) Bhowmick is a GIS professional, providing technical assistance for projects ranging in scale from the regional level to streetscapes. Heather earned an M.S. in Geographic Information Science for Development and Environment from Clark University and a B.S. in Economics-Finance and Quantitative Perspectives from Bentley University. She also completed a minor in Information Design and Corporate Communication while at Bentley. Using a variety of image processing and mapping software, she conducts spatial and statistical analyses to encourage compact development and natural resource preservation. In addition, she enjoys distilling complex geospatial concepts into straightforward infographics and web applications to effectively engage with and inform the public.
